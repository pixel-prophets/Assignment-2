World Energy Data

A multidimensional explorer of the Statistical Review of World Energy from the Energy Institute.

The parallel coordinates displays various metrics related to the production and consumption of energy, colored by the continent they belong to.

Controls

Brush: Drag vertically along an axis.
Remove Brush: Tap the axis background.
Reorder Axes: Drag a label horizontally.
Invert Axis: Tap an axis label.
Remove Axis: Drag axis label to the left edge.
